We offer $15 beginner classes every Friday at 6:00pm. Classes typically last around an hour and you get free rental shoes and a day pass!

New climbers get a free day pass and rental shoes when they come in with a member. Members can use one free pass a week.

Quick info

Learn more about our rules and fetures below!

  • Have work to do?

    Get it done here! We have a comfortable lounge area with free wi-fi for all your work related needs.

  • Jam out!

    Want to play your favorite song while you climb? Try our jukebox! We allow users to select the songs we play in the gym!

  • Heart healthy

    Did you know climbing is great cardio exercise? Remember to stay hydrated and refill your water bottle when needed!

  • Stay safe!

    Avoid walking under other climbers and stay at least an arms length away while on the wall. We're about fun, not injury!
